Live at CF Richmond Centre - Richmond Centre's Masterplan Redevelopment

Richmond is a point of pride in Metro Vancouver. A coastal city with outdoor beauty, diverse communities and a thriving metropolitan scene. One that’s closely linked to Vancouver by the SkyTrain and connected to the world by YVR. Its culture and history give it charm, while its continued growth, restaurants and international influence create an unmistakable energy.

At Richmond’s heart, CF Richmond Centre will transform from a world class shopping centre into a world class master planned destination. There will be something for everyone; boutique street front shopping districts that complement international brands. Tranquil green spaces nestled amongst the lively streets and a new central plaza. Even more exciting, over 2,000 new homes will be added, from studios to townhomes, offering lifestyles as diverse as the neighbourhoods they’re found in.

Today, Minoru Park, City Hall, SkyTrain’s Canada Line and CF Richmond Centre form the heart of the city. When complete, CF Richmond Centre will celebrate the character of Richmond among the features that define the world’s best cities.
